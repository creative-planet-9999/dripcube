Create irrigation
schedules automatically !

Input simple information about your garden, DripCube schedules
duration and frequency of irrigation for each zone. No more guess
work. Say good bye to over or under watering. Your garden will
thank you by becoming healthier and prettier.

You can of course decide between full automation or full manual
or something in between for each zondepending on your
preference and the special needs of your garden.

Automatically Adjusts
for Weather

DripCube Controllers monitor local weather every hour of every day and adjusts
watering so that you never have to worry about adjusting your irrigation schedules.
Adjustments are made not just for precipitation, but for temparature, humidity,
wind, due point, location and host of other variables based on established
science of calculating water needs of your plants tovarying weather.

Control from any
device anywhere

Once set, you can forget it. But if you ever have to check on your
irrigation - you don't have to fiddle with buttons and dials in a hot
garage or stuffy basement. You can do it from the comfort of your
coach using a device of your choice - a phone, a tablet, a laptop
or a desktop. And you can do it from anywhere in the world
where you can access the internet

Save Money, Save Water, Save Planet

resource it is and the planet will thank you for it! And your wallet will thank you too. DripCube pays for
itself during the very first year of its operation.
